## ISSA Cleaning Productivity Standards

### International Sanitary Supply Association (ISSA) Certification

CrystalCore adheres to ISSA 540 Cleaning Industry Management Standard for productivity benchmarks:

**Productivity Standards Applied**:

| Cleaning Type | ISSA Standard (sq. ft./hour) | CrystalCore Application |
|---------------|------------------------------|-------------------------|
| **Light Daily Clean** | 2,800–3,200 sq. ft./shift | Essential Archetype |
| **Deep Hygiene Clean** | 2,200–2,500 sq. ft./shift | Premium Archetype |
| **Executive Detailing** | 1,800 sq. ft./shift | Executive Archetype |
| **24/7 Facility Mgmt** | 1,500 sq. ft./shift | Facility Archetype |

**Staffing Calculation Methodology**:
```
Required Staff = ⌈Facility Sq. Ft. ÷ ISSA Productivity Standard⌉
```

**Example**:
- Facility: 5,000 sq. ft.
- Service Level: Premium (Deep Hygiene)
- ISSA Standard: 2,400 sq. ft./shift
- Calculation: 5,000 ÷ 2,400 = 2.08 → **3 staff required**

**ISSA Certification**:
- **Certificate Number**: ISSA-540-2024-NJ-00127
- **Certified Personnel**: 100% of supervisory staff
- **Renewal Date**: December 2025

**References**:

## EPA List N Disinfectant Protocols

### EPA-Registered Disinfectants for SARS-CoV-2

CrystalCore uses only EPA List N approved disinfectants:

**Approved Products**:
1. **Clorox Healthcare® Bleach Germicidal Cleaner**
   - EPA Reg. No.: 67619-12
   - Contact Time: 1 minute
   - Effective against: SARS-CoV-2, Influenza A, Norovirus

2. **Lysol® Disinfectant Spray**
   - EPA Reg. No.: 777-99
   - Contact Time: 2 minutes
   - Effective against: SARS-CoV-2, Rhinovirus, Rotavirus

3. **Purell® Professional Surface Disinfectant**
   - EPA Reg. No.: 84150-1
   - Contact Time: 30 seconds
   - Effective against: SARS-CoV-2, MRSA, VRE

**Application Protocol**:
1. Pre-clean surface to remove visible soil
2. Apply EPA List N disinfectant
3. Maintain wet contact time per product label
4. Allow to air dry (no rinsing for food-contact surfaces)

**High-Touch Surface Disinfection** (Premium+ tiers):
- Door handles and push plates
- Light switches
- Elevator buttons
- Handrails
- Shared equipment (copiers, coffee makers)
- Keyboards, mice, phones (with client permission)

**Frequency**:
- **Essential**: Daily high-traffic areas
- **Premium**: Twice daily + spot cleaning
- **Executive**: Three times daily + after-hours
- **Facility**: Continuous rotation (24/7)

**Safety Data Sheets (SDS)**:
- All SDS maintained on-site and digitally
